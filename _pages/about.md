BIO
======

As a second-year Ph.D. candidate in Computational Science, my research focuses in statistical modeling of time-stamped relational data, with a particular interest in extended relational event models. I am also deeply passionate about teaching and play an active role in outreach for the new BSc in Data Science program at USI. I consider myself determined and sunny, with a constant desire for learning new concepts.
